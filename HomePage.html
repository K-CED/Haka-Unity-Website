We come up with this company name owing to the fact that we are confident enough that by using this app called "Webby Devvy", you are going to learn more, especially in coding, and can help you to enhance your skills in programming. From the word "Hasaan", this app can help you to improve your concentration as a programmer, your expertness, and how to find ways to make something work, despite the odds. With the help of our application, we want to share knowledge to our fellow ICT students and enthusiast because we believe that it can be their steppingstone to learn new things about Information Technology.

MISSION:
Our mission is to develop an application that will provide educational content and necessary information about programming languages for the students, teachers, and other IT enthusiasts. We aim to make our app easy to use and accessible to everyone, and we guarantee to improve it further to appeal to the users so that they can share it with others.

VISION:
HAKA UNITY envisions itself to become a great provider of application that is dedicated to help every I.T students to learn easily and to improve and develop their skills. They will also be known in the Philippines for developing an effective and efficient learning for students.
